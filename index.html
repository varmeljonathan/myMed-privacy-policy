myMed Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 19, 2024

myMed is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal health information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data in our Android application.

1. Information We Collect

myMed collects and stores the following types of information:

1.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and account preferences
  • Profile Data: User preferences, settings, and app configuration

1.2 Health Information

  • Medication Data: Medication names, dosages, frequencies, and schedules
  • Health Metrics: Blood pressure readings, blood glucose levels, and other health measurements
  • Reminder History: Medication intake records and reminder responses

1.3 Device Information

  • App Usage: Features used, session duration, and interaction patterns
  • Device Data: Device type, Android version, and app version
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve the app

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: Provide medication tracking, scheduling, and reminder services
  • Data Management: Store and organize your health information securely on your device
  • Notifications: Send medication reminders and health alerts
  • Personalization: Customize the app experience based on your preferences
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and user experience
  • Support: Provide customer support and troubleshooting assistance

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: All your personal and health data is stored locally on your Android device. We do not store your sensitive health information on external servers.

3.1 Security Measures

  • Data encryption using Android's built-in security features
  • Secure local database storage
  • App-level security controls
  • Regular security updates

4. Data Sharing

We are committed to protecting your privacy:

  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal health information
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Your health data stays on your device and is not shared with third parties
  • Anonymous Analytics: Only anonymous, non-identifiable usage data may be collected to improve the app

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all your stored information within the app
  • Modify: Edit or update your personal and health information
  • Delete: Remove specific records or clear all app data
  • Export: Backup your data to external storage

6. Third-Party Services

myMed may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and updates
  • Firebase Analytics: For anonymous usage analytics (optional)
  • Android Notifications: For medication reminders

7. Children's Privacy

myMed is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we make changes:

  • We will post the updated policy
  • We will update the "Last updated" date
  • For significant changes, we will provide notice within the app

9. Permissions

myMed may request the following Android permissions:

  • Notifications: To send medication reminders
  • Storage: To save and backup your data locally
  • Network: For app updates and optional cloud backup
  • Boot Completed: To maintain reminders after device restart
